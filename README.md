The core promise is simple: instead of guessing what keywords or categories to use for your book, Publisher Rocket shows you real Amazon data — search volumes, competition scores, estimated earnings, and category rankings — so you can make decisions based on facts, not guesswork.

### **Publisher Rocket Key Features**

**1. Keyword Research**

- Finds high-demand, low-competition keywords used by real Amazon readers
- Shows monthly search volume
- Displays competitive score (0–100)
- Estimates potential monthly earnings per keyword

**2. Category Research**

- Explores 19,000+ Amazon book categories
- Shows number of books in each category
- Reveals sales needed to reach top 10
- Helps identify low-competition bestseller opportunities

- Analyzes top-ranking books for any keyword
- Displays estimated daily and monthly earnings

- Saves hours of manual keyword research

- Identifies keywords driving their sales
